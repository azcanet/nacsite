ABOUT US

We’re a fully community-funded grassroots organization, advocating for the interests of Canadian Azerbaijanis .The Network of Azerbaijani Canadians strives to represent the interests of Canadians of Azerbaijani origin

OUR TEAM

Our amazing Executive Director, Board Members, and Community Volunteers are committed to empowering and enriching the Azerbaitjani and Canadian communities. We’re here to turn our collective goals into action.

OUR MISSION

We focus on making the maximum positive change for the community. The Network of Azerbaijani Canadians strives to represent the interests of Canadians of Azerbaijani origin and make sure that the voice of our community is heard
